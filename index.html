About us

Import requirements of different countries are very complex. In order to ensure a smooth transport we arrange the whole transport process for you.

Respective pre export examinations, pre export quarantine, ground transportation, preparation of export health certificates and export customs documents, administrative formalities, flight bookings, optional insurance service, airport handling and the coordination with the consignee agent in the country of destination is part of our work as well as sending frozen horse semen, small pets like dogs and cats and horse and riding equipment. In the course of transport we are in constant contact with consignor, consignee, vet, laboratory, official veterinarian and client.

Our services

Roelofsen Raalte develops tailor-made transport solutions. From basic transport to luxurious multiple horse trucks. Trucks for branches of sport, custom built for you.

Our customers include show jumping riders, dressage riders, commercial stables, stud farms, but also recreational riders and riders practice disciplines such as drive, western riding and eventing.

The tailor-made transport solutions by Roelofsen go beyond the equestrion sports. We also build transport solutions for other sectors and target groups. For example we have customers in the race & rally sports, ice skating and motorcycling.
